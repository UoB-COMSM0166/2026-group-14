## Introduction
Defend London is a London-themed tower defense game in which players must protect the city’s most iconic landmarks from waves of invading enemies. Blending classic strategy gameplay with a distinctive illustrated art style, the game takes players across a stylized version of London, where each level presents a new defensive challenge inspired by famous locations and routes through the capital.

In Defend London, players face a mix of different enemy types, from fantasy-inspired creatures to unusual hostile forces, each requiring different tactical responses. To survive, players must place and upgrade a range of towers, manage resources carefully, and adapt their strategy as enemy waves become more intense. With missions such as The Outer Defenses, River Thames Patrol, and Tower of London Siege, the game combines recognizable London scenery with engaging tower defense mechanics to create a fun and memorable experience.
